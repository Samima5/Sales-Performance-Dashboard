Power BI Steps Used in This Project:

1. Data Import:
   - Cleaned CSV file imported into Power BI.

2. Data Transformation (Power Query):
   - Removed duplicates
   - Fixed data types
   - Created Month column
   - Created Region mapping

3. DAX Measures:
   - Total Sales = SUM(Sales)
   - Total Profit = SUM(Profit)
   - YoY Growth formula

4. Visuals Included:
   - Sales Trend (Line Chart)
   - Region-wise Sales (Bar Chart)
   - Top 5 Products (Column Chart)
   - KPI Cards

5. Final Output:
   - Interactive dashboard showing Sales Performance.
